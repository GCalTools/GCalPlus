# GCalPlus

Enhance your Google Calendar experience and boost your productivity with GCalPlus, the Chrome extension that adds powerful features to help you stay organized and efficient.

# GCalPlus Features Overview

## Look

- **Expand the Month Grid** — show at least twice as many events per day in Month view
- **Highlight & Toggle Busy/Free** — see busy/free status at a glance; adds a red/green Busy/Free toggle in event popups.
- **Show More All Day Events in Week/Day Views** — display twice as many all-day events.
- **Hide Invites on Shared Calendars** — hides the duplicate event shown on an invitee's shared calendar.
- **Hide Primary RSVPs for Secondary Calendar Events** — hides RSVP duplicates that appear on your primary calendar.
- **Hide Images** — removes the auto-added keyword illustrations.
- **Sort All Day Events** — list all-day events alphabetically.
- **Solid Tasks** — restores full task colours (ignores completed tasks).
- **Remove Task Circles** — more room for task titles.
- **Large Dots, Small Numbers** — compact headers and larger coloured bullets for timed events.
- **Hide Upgrade** — hides the Workspace upgrade button.
- **Colour Timed Events / Timed Bars** — colour timed event titles, or display like all-day bars with the time.
- **Weekend Color** — pick a colour for weekends.
- **Adj. Dates Color (M)** — background colour for adjacent-month dates in Month view.
- **Hide Adj. Dates (M / Y)** — hide adjacent-month dates and events in Month or Year view.
- **Today's Border** — choose colour and thickness for today's border.

## Feel

- **Disable Month Scrolling** — turns off month scrolling (pairs with *Expand the Month Grid*).
- **Use L/R Arrows** — navigate with arrow keys instead of `n`/`p`.
- **Expand Overlapping Timed Events** — hover to expand timed events to full width and bring them forward.
- **Show Titles/Descriptions on Hover** — popup preview when hovering events.
- **Disable Clicks on Location** — stops Maps opening when copying an event location.
- **Create Events from Year Previews** — adds a *Create Event* button when clicking days in Year view.
- **Multiple Copy** — adds a button to copy events to one or many dates across writable calendars, with optional guest copying.
- **Change Calendar in Event Popups** — move a single (non-repeating) event to another calendar from its popup.
- **Time Range** — hide everything before and after specified times.
